How to read the matrix
  • Filled / tinted cells — book harmony link between row and column spice.
  • Gold tint — shares at least one aroma group (reinforcing family).
  • Blue tint — harmony without shared group (complementary).
  • Dim cells — no harmony link in the extract.
  • Highlight — dims cells that do not involve the chosen spice.
  • Crosshair — hover highlights the row and column headers.
  • Stats line — Harmony pairs counts each unordered pair once (no double-count).
  • Tap a filled cell for side-by-side profiles.
